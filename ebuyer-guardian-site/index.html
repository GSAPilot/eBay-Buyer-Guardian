Three Risk Levels, One Glance

Hover any badge for a detailed breakdown of why a seller was scored the way they were.

Safe
GREEN
  • Feedback percentage meets or exceeds your threshold
  • Sufficient feedback count from other buyers
  • Account age is acceptable (not a brand new seller)
Caution
YELLOW
  • Feedback percentage is borderline or slightly below threshold
  • Low feedback count — few buyer reviews
  • Relatively new account with limited history
High Risk
RED
  • Feedback percentage critically below threshold
  • Very few or zero buyer reviews
  • Brand new or suspicious account — proceed with caution
