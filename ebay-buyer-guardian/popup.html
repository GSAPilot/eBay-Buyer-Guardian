eBay Buyer Guardian v1.1.0

Instantly spot risky eBay sellers with color-coded badges, customizable rules, and a history dashboard.

🟢 Green — Seller looks safe
🟡 Yellow — Some risk factors detected
🔴 Red — High risk, proceed with caution

How it works

  1. Visit any eBay listing or search results page
  2. The extension parses seller info locally (no external APIs)
  3. A color-coded badge appears next to the seller name
  4. Hover the badge for detailed risk breakdown (Pro)
  5. Customize rules in this popup (Pro)

Free vs Pro

Free: listing badges + basic risk scoring. Pro adds search badges, custom rules, detailed reasons, 500-history, CSV export, and premium badge styling.

Privacy

100% local. All analysis happens in your browser. License validation is the only external call (Lemon Squeezy). Seller history is stored in chrome.storage.sync.
