Three Risk Levels, One Glance

Hover any badge for a detailed breakdown of why a seller was scored the way they were. Green = safe, yellow = caution, red = high risk.

✅
Safe
GREEN
  • Feedback percentage meets or exceeds your threshold
  • Sufficient feedback count from buyers
  • Account age is acceptable (established seller)
⚠️
Caution
YELLOW
  • Feedback percentage is borderline or slightly below threshold
  • Low feedback count from buyers
  • Relatively new account with limited history
❌
High Risk
RED
  • Feedback percentage critically below threshold
  • Very few or no buyer reviews at all
  • Brand new or suspicious account with no track record
